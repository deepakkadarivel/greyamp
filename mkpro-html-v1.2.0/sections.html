Who we are

We are a team of diverse professionals with backgrounds in sales, consulting, marketing, project management, account management, and strategy, across various domains including FMCG, IT, software development and delivery, and telecom to name a few. The team as a whole has a combined experience of working in 15+ geographies, spanning 4 continents.
Greyamp Consulting is focussed on working with enterprises who have realised that digital transformation is the need of the hour. We enjoy the challenges each new engagement brings us, and also the opportunity to both learn, as well as share the knowledge we continue to accumulate.
Our modus operandi involves assessment of current state of the team or the organisation as a whole, mapping the current state of the talent and capability in the organisation, charting out the way forward, and enabling digital transformation through an in-depth and hands-on approach.
